MASTER BEDROOM

Why is it called the master bedroom? It's because a householder, if male, was known as the Master, the abbreviation for which is Mr. His wife the mistress of the house has the abbreviation Mrs. The master bedroom is the one used by the Master or the Master and his wife. ... The “master bedroom” was used in architectural plans to mean the owner's bedroom.
